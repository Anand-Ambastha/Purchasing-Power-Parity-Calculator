About IMF PPP Data

The International Monetary Fund (IMF) provides the most accurate and up-to-date Purchasing Power Parity (PPP) conversion rates. These rates are based on a comprehensive basket of goods and services and are updated quarterly in the World Economic Outlook database. This ensures the most accurate comparison of purchasing power across different countries.
